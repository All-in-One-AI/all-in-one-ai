WPAI is a powerful WordPress plugin that utilizes artificial intelligence to enhance the functionality and performance of your website. This plugin integrates seamlessly with your WordPress installation and provides intelligent features to optimize user experience and streamline website management.

=== Features ===

* Intelligent Content Recommendations: WP AI analyzes user behavior and content patterns to offer personalized recommendations for related articles, products, or services.
* Automated Tagging: The plugin automatically suggests relevant tags for your posts based on their content, saving you time and effort in the tagging process.
* Smart SEO Optimization: WP AI optimizes your website's SEO by analyzing your content and providing suggestions for improving meta tags, headings, and other SEO elements.
* Natural Language Processing: This plugin employs natural language processing techniques to understand user queries and provide accurate and contextually relevant search results.
* Advanced Analytics: Gain valuable insights into user behavior, popular content, and engagement metrics with WP AI's comprehensive analytics dashboard.

=== Installation ===

To install WP AI on your WordPress website, follow these steps:

1. Download the WP AI plugin from the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/andbalashov/wpai).
2. Extract the downloaded ZIP file to your local machine.
3. Connect to your WordPress website using FTP or any other file management tool.
4. Navigate to the `wp-content/plugins` directory in your WordPress installation.
5. Upload the extracted `wpai` folder to the `plugins` directory.
6. Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard.
7. Go to the **Plugins** page.
8. Locate the WP AI plugin and click the **Activate** button to enable the plugin.

=== Configuration ===

Once the WP AI plugin is activated, you need to configure it to take full advantage of its features. Here's how you can configure the plugin:

1. Navigate to the **Settings** menu in your WordPress admin dashboard.
2. Click on **WP AI** to access the plugin's settings page.
3. Adjust the settings according to your preferences. These settings may include content recommendations, SEO optimization, and analytics integration.
4. Save your changes.

=== Usage ===

WP AI provides an intuitive and user-friendly interface for managing its features. Here's a brief overview of how to use the plugin:

=== Content Recommendations ===

To enable intelligent content recommendations, make sure the feature is enabled in the plugin settings. Once enabled, WP AI will automatically display related articles, products, or services based on user behavior and content patterns.

=== Automated Tagging ===

WP AI simplifies the process of adding tags to your posts. When creating or editing a post, the plugin will suggest relevant tags based on the content of your post. You can choose to accept these suggestions or add your own tags.

=== Smart SEO Optimization ===

To optimize your website's SEO, WP AI offers suggestions for improving meta tags, headings, and other SEO elements. You can access these suggestions through the plugin's settings page or directly in the post editor when editing a post.

=== Natural Language Processing ===

WP AI utilizes natural language processing techniques to provide accurate and contextually relevant search results. By integrating with the default WordPress search functionality, WP AI enhances the search experience for your website visitors.

=== Advanced Analytics ===

WP AI includes a comprehensive analytics dashboard that provides valuable insights into user behavior, popular content, and engagement metrics. You can access the analytics dashboard by navigating to the **WP AI** section in your WordPress admin dashboard.
